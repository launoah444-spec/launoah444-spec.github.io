About FUZHOU HEART

Discovering the beauty, culture and history of Fuzhou.

Who We Are

FUZHOU HEART is a modern website project dedicated to introducing the city of Fuzhou — the historic capital of Fujian Province, China. Our goal is to showcase the city's culture, history, architecture, language, and natural beauty through a clean and immersive digital experience.

History

Fuzhou has more than 2,200 years of history and was once the capital of several historical kingdoms and dynasties.

Culture

From Min Opera to traditional architecture and tea culture, Fuzhou preserves a rich cultural heritage.

Nature

Mountains, rivers, hot springs and coastlines make Fuzhou one of the most beautiful cities in Southeast China.
